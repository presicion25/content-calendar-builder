📖 How to Use

Everything you need to get the most out of Content Calendar Builder.

🚀 Quick Start
1
Add your API key
Go to ⚙️ API Settings and paste your Claude, OpenAI, or Gemini key. The AI is called directly from your browser — your key never touches any other server.
2
Describe your niche and platform
Enter your niche (e.g. "sustainable fashion" or "B2B SaaS"), choose your platform, and select how many days per week you want to post.
3
Hit Build Calendar
The AI generates a 30-day content plan mapped to your content pillars. Edit any idea inline, lock the ones you love, then export or save to library.
📅 Calendar Builder
✏️
Edit Inline
Click any idea text on the calendar to edit it directly. Changes save automatically to your session.
🔒
Lock Posts
Click the lock icon on any post to protect it from being overwritten when you regenerate.
🏷️
Track Status
Mark each post as Draft, Scheduled, Published, or Skip using the status badge on each card.
♻️
Regenerate
Get fresh AI ideas for all unlocked posts without disturbing the ones you've locked.
📤
Export
Download as PDF, Word doc, or iCal to import directly into your scheduling tool or calendar app.
🗓️
Future Months
Change the month selector to any future date, build, then save — plan 3–6 months ahead at once.
🏛️ Content Pillars

Pillars are recurring content categories that give your feed structure and balance. The AI assigns each post day a pillar and generates an idea to match.

📚 Educate — Teach something useful, share a tip, explain a concept
🎙️ Story — Share personal experience, lessons, wins, or failures
🏆 Authority — Build credibility: opinions, data, frameworks, proof
🤝 Community — Spark conversation, ask questions, engage your audience
🚀 Promote — Share your offer, product, or win — soft or direct
Use the Pillar Weights sliders to control how often each pillar appears. A typical balanced mix skews toward Educate + Story with occasional Promote.
🎙️ Voice Analyzer

Builds a brand voice profile so the AI writes content in your style rather than generic output. Two ways to create a profile:

🤖 AI Analyze
Paste 3–5 of your best posts or your full bio. The AI reads them and detects your tone, cadence, audience, content style, and goal automatically. Best for established creators with existing content.
✍️ Describe My Voice
Use the dropdowns to manually select your tone, cadence, brand voice, content style, audience, and goal. Great if you're just starting out or want precise control over every attribute.
After building a profile, click Apply to Calendar to push those settings into the Context row, then hit Regenerate to get voice-matched ideas. Save multiple profiles for different platforms under 📋 Saved Voice Profiles.
📚 Saved Calendars

Archive complete months with all edits, locks, and statuses intact. Load any saved month to pick up exactly where you left off.

1
Build your calendar for any month
Set the month selector to a future date, generate, edit, and finalize the content.
2
Click 📚 Save to Library in the actions bar
The entire calendar — edits, locks, statuses — is saved to your browser's local storage.
3
Load from Saved Calendars
Open 📚 Saved Calendars from the sidebar and click Load on any entry to restore it completely.

💡 Pro Tips
→Run the Voice Analyzer before building your first calendar. The Context settings it fills in make a significant difference in the quality of generated ideas.
→Build 3–6 months at once by changing the month selector, generating, then saving each to library before moving to the next.
→Lock your best ideas before regenerating so you never lose a line you liked.
→Save separate voice profiles for each platform (e.g. "LinkedIn — Professional", "Instagram — Casual") and load the right one before each build.
→Export to iCal and import into Google Calendar, Notion, or any scheduling tool to see your content plan alongside everything else on your plate.
→The 💾 Save button auto-saves your current session so you can close the tab and return without losing anything. It restores automatically on next open.
